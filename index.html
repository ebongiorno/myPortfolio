About

Hi I'm Emily! I'm a 2nd year Computer Science major at Cal State Fullerton. I first got into computers from a middle school class and have been facinated ever since. When asked why I chose my major, I tell people that I like creating. I like being able to type something out and have it do things on the screen. That might lead more towards game design, but I've always felt more interested in playing games than making them. I hope to make a carreer out of software in this ever-changing world and make some cool things along the way.

When not figuring out endless bugs in programs, I enjoy reading and playing games. Choosing to loose myself in fantasy worlds to relax outside the lines of code. I like taking characters and thinking about other situations to throw them in outside their normal worlds to create even more interesting stories.
